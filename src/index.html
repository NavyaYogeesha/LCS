About Us

We take pleasure in introducing ourselves as group of professionals in the institution of food service business. M/s. Lakshmi Catering Service a Dedicated institution food serving company has established a state of the art Production (Kitchen) facility at M/s Lakshmi Catering Services.

We have a team of well - trained chefs carrying experience between six to fifteen years in Food and Beverage production to create a palatable experience in North Indian, South Indian, and Chinese, we in the past have been appreciated for theme Cuisine in particular North, South Indian as well as Chinese dishes.

Since 2002, Partners have strategically located this new facility to cater to the needs of in and around Electronic City, Bommasandra and Veersandra.

Our experience has enabled us to identify the key factor for the success of an institutions food service company and we take great care to address each of these and thus be able to give you the best "Value for Money".

Finally we need an opportunity to prove our young talents in supply of quality food in time with hygienic conditions.
